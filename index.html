ABOUT ME

My career has revolved around the captivating world of UI design, and three years ago, I embarked on an enchanting journey by launching my handmade candle business. Additionally, I've had the incredible opportunity to engage with people and provide customer service for a delightful eight years, fostering a genuine empathy for the human experience.
Right now, I'm in the midst of an exhilarating UX/UI Bootcamp at Monash University, juggling multiple exciting projects. I've always been drawn to human-centered design, and there's nothing I enjoy more than solving real-life problems with creative solutions.
Looking ahead, my goal is to dive even deeper into the realms of user psychology and human-computer interaction. I firmly believe in the power of continuous learning to bolster my skills in the dynamic field of UX/UI. Feel free to explore the rest of my portfolio and uncover the various projects and topics I've had the privilege of working on and developing.
